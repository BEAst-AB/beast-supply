[[price]]
= Price

Used to provide information about price when the buyer has a need to get this information prior to the invoice. 
Common use cases is goods and services that are difficult to set a price in a catalogue in advance but also when the buyer wants 
to check price as well as the quantity of the goods and services prior to the invoice.
